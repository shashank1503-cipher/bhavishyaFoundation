About Us

Who Are We

Bhavishya Foundation is an Non-Profit Organisation primary run by college students which is currently active in Lucknow . Our organisation strives to eliminate hunger in children of all ages. Alongside,Bhavishya Foundation also aims at helping animals.
We provide help by giving out good nutritious food and we also provide aid to street animals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help as many people as we can and make INDIA a country with lowest rate of malnourished children. We also aim to save and feed street dogs.



Our Vision

Bhavishya Foundation has a vision of great INDIA where no poor little soul suffers due to hunger or poverty. We want to help the helpless in every way we can because they say 'Not for ourselves alone are we born.'
